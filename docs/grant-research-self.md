**Helyzet:** főállás mellett indítandó, AI-alapú termék + tanácsadás cég; nincs még cég; nincs árbevétel/lezárt év; cél non-dilutive PÁLYÁZATI tőke.

### 2/10 — NEM jogosult

**Vállalkozóvá válást elősegítő támogatás (NFSZ statutory labour-market program: 6 months of wage-replacement at 100% of minimum wage + one-time non-repayable "tőketámogatás" for newly starting businesses), administered by the vármegyei kormányhivatal / járási hivatal foglalkoztatási osztálya. NOT the closed "Vállalkozó Start" GINOP/OFA variant.**

- **Illeszkedés:** Financially this is one of the very few Hungarian non-dilutive vehicles explicitly built for a brand-new, zero-revenue company (the firm must NOT yet exist / must be newly founded — no closed business year or revenue is required, which normally is the killer filter for new founders). 100% non-repayable in the standard form, no önerő, ~5M Ft total (322,800 Ft/mo x 6 ≈ 1.94M Ft income replacement + up to 3,000,000 Ft one-time capital grant in 2026 county calls; statutory cap is 15x minimum wage ≈ 4.84M Ft). Entity can be új egyéni vállalkozó or a new micro Kft/Bt; KATA generally permitted. The capital part can cover startup investment/equipment and (in the old Vállalkozó Start catalogue) explicitly covered IT/software/website/AI-type dev and a consulting setup — so the spend profile would plausibly fit. BUT the program is reserved for registered jobseekers (nyilvántartott álláskereső for at least 1 month at application), with a 30+ age rule in most county calls. A person in full-time employment is by definition NOT an álláskereső and is therefore INELIGIBLE. The whole program logic is to move an unemployed person into self-employment as their MAIN activity and keep the business running to the end of the support term — a side-business kept alongside a day job does not fit at all. Verified across NFSZ official pages (cikk/82, cikk/1596) and the Feb-2026 Baranya county hirdetmény. Decisive mismatch with this founder's employed/side-business situation.

- **Összeg / intenzitás:** ~5M Ft total: 322,800 Ft/month x 6 months (≈1.94M Ft income replacement) + up to 3,000,000 Ft one-time non-repayable capital grant in 2026 county calls (statutory ceiling 15x minimum wage ≈ 4.84M Ft; counties set lower practical caps). 100% non-repayable in the standard form (some county capital components may be repayable or need collateral — verify per county).

- **Határidő / státusz:** OPEN in 2026 but rolling, county-by-county, budget-limited — no single national deadline. Confirmed live activity: Feb-2026 hirdetmények in Baranya and Bács-Kiskun; Pest, Heves, Tolna, Zala, Nógrád referenced as open through spring 2026 (verified June 2026). Availability rotates as county frames run out — must confirm directly with your own járási hivatal foglalkoztatási osztálya. (The named "Vállalkozó Start" Budapest/Pest variant is CLOSED: intake ended 16 Oct 2020, program wound down 2024 — do not target it.)

- **0. lépés (mi kéne a jogosultsághoz):** Step 0 (and it defeats the "on-the-side" goal): QUIT the day job; register at the járási hivatal foglalkoztatási osztálya as a jobseeker (nyilvántartott álláskereső) and stay registered for AT LEAST 1 month; meet the age 30+ threshold most county calls require (under-30 -> Ifjúsági Garancia track). Then complete the mandatory ~1-month group business-counselling course (vállalkozási tanácsadás), prepare a business plan and get it approved to receive the Tanúsítvány. Only then submit the support kérelem (paper or via e-Papír) to the járási hivatal in your own county of residence/seat. After approval, register the new company (új ev or new Kft/Bt; do not pre-register it) and keep it running to the end of the support period (clawback if abandoned early). Check the specific county hirdetmény for whether the capital component is repayable and whether collateral/önerő is required. The business must be the founder's main activity, not a side gig.

- **Fő bökkenő:** Reserved for registered jobseekers (nyilvántartott álláskereső for at least 1 month; usually age 30+). A full-time employed founder is by definition not a jobseeker and is INELIGIBLE; the program requires self-employment to be the MAIN activity, so a side-business kept alongside a day job is structurally excluded. Accessing it would require quitting the job and registering as unemployed first — which defeats the entire "on-the-side" premise.
